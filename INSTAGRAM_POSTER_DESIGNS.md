# Oakstratton Instagram Poster Designs

## 📐 Instagram Specifications

### Feed Posts
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1350 px
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:5
- **Safe Zone**: Keep critical content within 1000x1250px
- **Text Readability**: Minimum 24px font size

### Stories
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16
- **Safe Zone**: Keep critical content within 1000x1800px

### Carousel Posts
- **Same as Feed Posts** (1080 x 1350px per slide)
- Recommended: 3-5 slides per carousel

---

## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary Cyan**: #00BAEC (CTAs, accents)
- **Secondary Purple**: #6B5DD6 (Benefits, impact)
- **Tertiary Pink**: #FF6B9D (Merchant benefits)
- **Dark Teal**: #1A4D5C (Headlines, text)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (Clean backgrounds)
- **Light Gray**: #F5F5F5 (Secondary backgrounds)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold sans-serif (48-56px)
- **Body Copy**: Regular sans-serif (28-32px)
- **Captions**: Light sans-serif (20-24px)
- **Font Family**: Inter, Poppins, or similar modern sans-serif

### Logo Usage
- Oakstratton brain/circuit logo: 120-150px
- Always include on bottom right corner
- Minimum 20px padding from edges
- Use white or dark version depending on background contrast

## 📸 Image/Visual Element Placement

### For Each Poster, Consider Adding:

1. **Subtle Background Elements**
   - Geometric shapes (circles, lines)
   - Circuit board patterns (tie to brain logo)
   - Leaf/organic elements (sustainability angle)
   - Keep opacity at 5-10% for readability

2. **Icon Usage**
   - Payment method icons (Klarna, Clearpay, PayPal)
   - Conversion/growth arrow icons
   - Security/lock icons (trust)
   - User/people icons (community)
   - Dollar/money icons (transactions)

3. **Image Assets Needed**
   - Professional business photo of diverse merchants (optional)
   - Product/service images that BNPL can be used for
   - Abstract payment/transaction visuals
   - Success/celebration imagery

## 🎯 Design Best Practices for Instagram

1. **Mobile-First**: Design for phone viewing first
2. **Text Contrast**: Ensure 7:1 contrast ratio (WCAG AAA)
3. **Whitespace**: Don't overcrowd—breathing room matters
4. **Brand Consistency**: Use Oakstratton colors and logo placement
5. **Call-to-Action**: Always include clear next step
6. **Hashtag Strategy**: Mix trending + branded hashtags
7. **Posting Time**: Post when your audience is most active
8. **Story Stickers**: Use polls, questions, CTAs in stories
9. **Carousel Flow**: Each slide should be standalone + part of narrative
10. **Testing**: A/B test variations to find top performers
